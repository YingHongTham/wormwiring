White data

Data of White et al., 1986, The Structure of the Nervous System of the Nematode Caenorhabditis elegans (The Mind of a Worm) Phil. Trans. R. Soc. Lond. B, 314, 1-340.
Available through WormAtlas.org: https://wormatlas.org/MoW_built0.92/MoW.html
Skeleton and some volumetric maps of all neuron classes and most individual neurons. Connections of ventral neuron motor neurons by class. Drawings of cell body positions and neuron tracts. Chemical and gap junction synapses. Number of synapses given by class.
